What Is a Bean Pie?

Before diving into what makes Shaba's Bean Pies stand out, let's first talk about the star of the show: the bean pie. Originating in African American communities, particularly in the South, bean pies are a delicious blend of sweet and savory ingredients, with the key ingredient being navy beans. When combined with a mixture of spices, sugar, and a buttery crust, the result is a rich and flavorful dessert that can be enjoyed at any time of day.

While bean pies have a long history and are tied to soul food culture, Shaba's Bean Pies has taken this traditional treat to the next level with a recipe that has won the hearts and taste buds of many.
